# 🔮 Ask your question! Magic Ball for Garmin

A simple and fun Garmin smartwatch app that brings the classic **Magic 8 Ball** experience to your wrist.

Ask a question, tap the screen, and receive a random prediction — whether you need guidance, motivation, or just a bit of fun.

## Features

- 🎱 Random prediction generation
- ⌚ Designed specifically for Garmin smartwatches
- ⚡ Fast and lightweight experience
- 🎨 Simple and intuitive interface
- 🌙 Optimized for wearable screens

## How to Use

1. Open the Magic Ball app on your Garmin watch.
2. Think of a question.
3. Shake the watch or tap the screen.
4. Reveal your prediction.
